HI... I AM TUSHAR

I'm a front-end developer and a full-stack development fresher with a keen interest in photography and data analysis.My journey in tech began with my MCA from ITER SOA, where I honed my skills in software development.
Alongside coding, I have a passion for capturing the world through my lens, blending creativity with technical expertise. I believe in the power of data to tell stories, just as much as a photograph can capture a moment. Whether I'm coding, analyzing data, or taking photos, I'm driven by curiosity and a desire to create. Here, you’ll find a collection of my favorite shots, each one a piece of my journey. ~ MY STORY ~

I'm a tech enthusiast with a passion for capturing moments through photography. Ever since I can remember, I've been drawn to the beauty of the world around me.Whether it was the vibrant colors of a sunset, the intricate patterns of a leaf, or the candid moments of everyday life, I found myself wanting to capture these scenes.
This desire led me to pick up a camera, and that's where my love for photography began.
Although I’m not a professional photographer, photography has become a meaningful part of my life. I started by taking simple photos with my phone, experimenting with angles, lighting, and composition. Over time, I discovered how a single image could tell a story, evoke emotions, and preserve memories.
